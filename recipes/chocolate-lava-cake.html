Chocolate Lava Cake

This chocolate lava cake recipe is an absolute showstopper. With a crispy outer shell and a gooey, molten chocolate center, it is the ultimate indulgent dessert. Ready in under 30 minutes, it is perfect for date nights, dinner parties, or whenever you need a chocolate fix. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for the full experience.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Serving Yield
10 mins 12 mins 22 mins 4 4 cakes

Cooking Options for Lava Cake

These lava cakes are baked in the oven in this recipe, but they can also be made in an air fryer or microwave for a quicker result. The key is not to overbake — the center should remain soft and molten.

  • Oven: Preheat the oven to 425℉. Grease ramekins, fill with batter, and bake for 12 minutes until edges are firm but center is still jiggly.
  • Air Fryer: Preheat the air fryer to 370℉. Place filled ramekins inside and air fry for 10 minutes. Let rest 1 minute before inverting.
  • Microwave: Pour batter into a greased mug and microwave on high for 60–75 seconds. The center should look slightly underdone — it will set as it cools slightly.

What to Serve With Chocolate Lava Cake

Lava cakes pair beautifully with a wide range of accompaniments. A classic scoop of vanilla ice cream on the side creates a wonderful hot-and-cold contrast. Fresh raspberries or strawberries add a fruity tartness that balances the rich chocolate. Dust the top with powdered sugar or drizzle with caramel sauce for extra elegance.

For a more decadent presentation, serve alongside whipped cream, a sprinkle of sea salt flakes, or a shot of espresso on the side.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 6 ounces semi-sweet dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425℉. Grease four ramekins with butter and lightly dust with cocoa powder or flour. Place them on a baking sheet.

Step 2: Melt butter and chopped chocolate together in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until fully smooth. Stir in powdered sugar until combined.

Step 3: Whisk in eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract. Fold in flour and salt until just combined — do not overmix.

Step 4: Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins. Bake for 12 minutes, until the edges are set but the center is still soft. Let rest for 1 minute, then carefully invert onto serving plates.

Step 5: Serve immediately with ice cream or desired toppings and enjoy!
